Common questions

Everything tradies usually ask before signing up.

How is this different from Xero?

Xero is built for accountants. PayDay Tradie is built for tradies who quote, invoice, and chase payments between jobs — without an accountant translating it. We focus on the daily realities (job costing, GST set aside, late payment chasing) rather than full general ledger accounting.

Does it handle BAS and GST?

Yes. GST is automatically set aside as you invoice, so by the time BAS lands you already know what to pay. Quarterly BAS summaries are built in — in plain English, not accountant jargon.

Can I use it on site?

Yes. It works on phone, ute laptop, or office desktop. Snap a photo of a receipt at the supplier, send an invoice from a job site, mark a job complete on the way home.

What about my accountant?

Keep them. PayDay Tradie keeps your records organised so they spend less time cleaning up and more on tax planning. You can export reports anytime your accountant needs them.

Where is my data stored?

Right now your data lives only in your browser on this device — nothing is sent off your machine. When full sync launches, you’ll be able to opt in to back up across devices and crew.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. 30 days of full Business-tier access — payroll, reports, approvals, the lot — with no credit card required. After 30 days you choose Starter, Crew, Business, or Custom.
